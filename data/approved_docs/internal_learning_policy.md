## Learning Time

Learners should receive realistic study plans based on work capacity. High workload learners should not be assigned excessive study hours.

## Study Planning Rules

- High workload learners should receive shorter sessions.
- Learners with fewer than 12 focus hours per week should receive lighter weekly plans.
- Learners with 15 or more focus hours per week can receive longer study blocks.
- Study plans should include weekly checkpoints.
- Study plans should adapt if a learner is not ready.
- Study plans should account for difficulty and prerequisites.

## Manager Visibility

Managers should receive team-level summaries rather than sensitive learner-level details.

Acceptable manager insights:
- Team readiness percentage
- Skill gap trends
- Certification risk areas
- Capacity risk by team
- Suggested support actions

Avoid exposing:
- Personal details
- Private schedules
- Individual performance details without need
- Any real employee or customer information

## Human Review

If an agent output is missing citations, exposes sensitive data, recommends unrealistic study hours, or makes unsupported claims, it should be flagged for human review.
